story so far

Conversion Principles Built By Experience.

We have been working on opensource and closedsource software projects for 5 years, along with 10 years of marketing experience for fortune 100 companies. Most of my work centers around "UGC" or "User Generated Content" for organic growth.

2013

Won idea pitch competition in college for UGC and recruited to Goldman Sachs. Started a wordpress agency, which was later acquired by an agency.

2015

Started as VP of Technology at an ad agency. Introduced ideas about modern tracking of affiliate points.

2016

Promoted to CTO, created “Terri” Our landing page + tracking app. Since we were spending tens of millions in ad spend between 17 clients, Terri managed creation of dynamic landing pages and tracking of each click down to the individual level.

2017

Terri was featured by Cloudflare and Google in case studies. Specifically, our ability to remove to the amp.google domain from our pages in order to maintain our individual user tracking.

2018

Presented our individual user tracking system at Google

2019

Quit my job to make software for nonprofits in SEAsia

2020

Spent a significant amount of time an capital testing marketing software ideas. Many died, many were launched live in production. Food delivery app, luxury hotels, custom webapps and more UGC testing. Corona significantly effected our revenue streams, and we had to start looking for other sources of revenue.

2021

Started on UPWORK and focused on shopify ecommerce, UGC played vital roles in our success (Last year we say 800% increase in order volume since starting our UGC programs).

2022

Maintained effective UGC shopify clients, but moving back into crypto space. Take the past almost 10 years of focus on UGC and conversion optimization and funneling it into a product we own.

I’m not running, this is stuff I’ve talked about forever. While concept names have changed UGC, CRO, Brand Loyalty, Affiliate), the core remains the same… gather unique organic content, deliver it quickly, and engage with your audience. I believe our UGC crypto app could take all of what I’ve learned and channel it into a product I’ve always dreamed of. I’ll stay focused… I’ve already done my 3 years of test and fail :)

Paul Chris Luke, Founder
